It’s not that a quiet mind makes us happy, but that a content mind is naturally settled.

And nothing brings contentment to the mind like understanding. It craves knowing what’s going on. And it will continue to spin, looking for answers, until it finds genuine solutions.

The better it understands, the happier it is.

So the purpose of these programs is to satisfy the mind’s thirst for understanding.

This can happen through: revealing stories, engaged discussion, thought provoking questions, playful reflections, insightful meditation.

Through, and after, each class people find themselves happier, calmer, and refreshed. And drop by drop, over time, this happy mind becomes a habit.
